FILIPINO ART MASTERS

FERNANDO AMORSOLO

The first-ever National Artist of the Philippines, Amorsolo is celebrated for his mastery of light and his ability to capture the soul of the Filipino countryside.

JUAN LUNA

A giant of the 19th-century art scene, his works served as a catalyst for Filipino nationalism and earned international acclaim in Europe.

GELONIMATION

A modern pioneer in Filipino storytime animation, blending relatable humor with a distinct digital art style that defines the new generation.

ABE ANIMATED

Leading the wave of independent animation in the Philippines with high-quality, emotive storytelling and fluid character movements.
