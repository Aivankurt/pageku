FILIPINO ART MASTERS

FERNANDO AMORSOLO

Fernando Amorsolo was the first-ever National Artist of the Philippines. Known for his mastery of light, his legacy lives on in the National Museum and private collections.

JUAN LUNA

Juan Luna was a giant of 19th-century art. His masterpiece, the 'Spoliarium', won gold at the Madrid Exposition and remains a symbol of Filipino pride and history.

GELONIMATION

Gelonimation is a pioneer of the "Storytime Animation" genre in the Philippines, known for humorous local relatable content that resonates with millions.

ABE ANIMATED

Abe Animated is a prominent figure in the local independent animation scene, focusing on heartfelt and artistic storytelling through modern digital media.
