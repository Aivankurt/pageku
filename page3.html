FILIPINO ART MASTERS

GELONIMATION

Gelonimation is a pioneer of the "Storytime Animation" genre in the Philippines, known for humorous local relatable content.

ABE ANIMATED

Abe Animated is a prominent figure in the local independent animation scene, focusing on heartfelt and artistic storytelling.

FERNANDO AMORSOLO

The first-ever National Artist of the Philippines, known for his mastery of light and rural landscapes.
